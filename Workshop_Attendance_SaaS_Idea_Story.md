# 📘 A Simple Explanation of the Idea We Are Building

## Introduction

I want to work on an idea that solves a **real, daily problem** faced by
workshops and service businesses.

Imagine I own a **vehicle workshop**. I have multiple employees working
every day. Some of them stay inside the workshop, while others often go
outside for **on-site or roadside repairs** when a vehicle breaks down
nearby.

Managing all this manually becomes very difficult.

---

## The Problem I Face Today

Every day, I struggle with questions like:

- Who came to work on time?
- Who left early?
- Who actually worked how many hours?
- Which employee went outside for repairs, and for how long?
- How much time did they spend traveling?
- Did someone really do overtime, or are they just saying it?
- How do I calculate salaries fairly?
- What if an employee asks for salary advance?

Most workshops still use: - Manual registers - WhatsApp messages - Phone
calls - Guesswork

This leads to **confusion, disputes, and loss of trust**.

---

## What I Want to Build

I want to build a **simple but powerful system** that automatically
handles all of this.

The idea is to create: - One **mobile app** for employees - One **web
dashboard** for the workshop owner

Everything should be **automatic, transparent, and reliable**.

---

## How Attendance Will Work

Each workshop has a fixed location.

When an employee: - **enters the workshop area**, the system
automatically marks them as **present** - **leaves the workshop**, the
system understands they are no longer working there

No punching, no registers, no excuses.

---

## What About On-Site / Roadside Work?

Many times, employees need to go outside the workshop for repairs.

So instead of guessing, the system works like this:

- The owner assigns a repair job to an employee
- The employee travels to the repair location
- The system tracks:
  - Travel time
  - Actual repair work time
- Once the job is completed, everything is recorded automatically

This way, **travel time and repair time are both counted properly**.

---

## Salary and Overtime

At the end of the day or month, the system already knows:

- How long the employee worked in the workshop
- How long they traveled
- How long they worked on-site
- Whether they did overtime or not

Based on this, salary is calculated **fairly and transparently**.

No more arguments. Everyone sees the same data.

---

## Salary Advance Feature

Sometimes employees need money in advance.

Instead of verbal requests:

- The employee sends an **advance request** from the mobile app
- The workshop owner sees it on the dashboard
- The owner can **approve or reject**
- Approved advances are adjusted in the final salary

Everything is recorded properly.

---

## Making This a Product for Others

This problem is not just mine.

Almost **every workshop or service business** faces the same issues.

So instead of building this only for one workshop, the idea is to make
it a **software product** that:

- Any workshop can sign up for
- Add their employees
- Use the same system

They will pay a **monthly fee per employee**, which makes the pricing
simple and fair.

---

## How the System Will Be Used

### Workshop Owner

- Uses the web dashboard
- Adds employees
- Assigns jobs
- Approves salary advances
- Views attendance and salary reports

### Employees

- Use the mobile app
- Attendance is automatic
- Start and end repair jobs
- Request salary advances
- View their own work history

---

## Why This Makes Sense

- Saves time
- Reduces disputes
- Builds trust
- Works automatically
- Easy to explain and use
- Solves a real business problem

---

## Final Thought

This is not just an app idea.

It is a **practical solution** to a daily operational problem that
exists in thousands of workshops and service centers.

The goal is to build something: - Simple to use - Fair for employees -
Helpful for owners - Scalable for the future
